Admission Information

Admission Criteria

  • Minimum 50% marks in Intermediate (FSc / ICS or equivalent)
  • Pass university entry test
  • Interview (if required)

Application Process

  1. Fill out online application form
  2. Submit required documents
  3. Appear in entry test
  4. Wait for merit list
  5. Pay fee to confirm admission
